Checking in again on some updates after a 14-day streak of practicing the Wim Hof Method.

I haven't noticed any negative effects so far of practicing the Wim Hof Method -- including the breathing method, cold water immersion, and focus / concentration.

I did have some concerns in that after 4-6 rounds of breathing, I would experience a ringing in the ears, which gradually grew louder, as well as tingling in my hands. However, I've since learned that this is a positive affect of oxygen saturation in the blood from the method.

This helpful PDF clarifies some of the intense effects felt during the breathing practice.

Some positives:

  • I'm losing weight. This is likely a combination of the cold water immersion (which burns fat), and an increased motivation to exercise.
  • I haven't reacted to my wife with anger or frustration during times I normally would.
  • The "blues" that I normally experience (especially during the fall season) aren't happening.
  • Increased desire to pursue my teenage & childhood loves. I bought a guitar and began playing music again (I used to play every day).
